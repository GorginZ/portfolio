Cabbage Moths

2/03/20
Kale is the most nutritious vegetable known to man, probably. Cabbage moths absolutely love it. Every day I see the white moth dancing around my garden landing to dispatch an egg or 2000 on my tuscan kale. One time I saw a wasp, sussing out the kale, I think it knew it could find some tasty catterpillars there. Until I can lure some ladybugs with my seeding parsley I pick them off and feed them to my small but carnivorous fish - the problem with this strategy is that the fish is so small, so once they're a certain size they can pass a 'TOO BIG TO FAIL' threshold and at this point they are mercifully thrown into another part of my garden.
