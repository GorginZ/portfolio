Digital Piano Circuit Boards

1/4/20
Digital pianos are just computers. If you can work your way through the schematic you have a good shot at trouble shooting your 20 year old terrible digital piano you just got given for free. This particular piano had 8 keys fail. Initially we assumed the contacts were dusty, but cleaning them out fixed nothing. We poured hours into understanding the schematic and using a volt-meter to figure out which components were low or abnormal. We identified a resistor capacitor circuit that was to blame, unforunately without a very small tipped soldering iron, it is unlikely we will be able to fix it, and I'd never bother pay for anyone else to fix it.
